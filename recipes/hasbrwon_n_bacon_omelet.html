Hashbrowns and bacon omelete

Description

Hash browns make a delicious crust for grab-and-go egg bites made in a muffin tin.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 3 cups of frozen potatoes
  • 3tbsp of butter
  • 1/8 tbso of salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 cups of shredded mexican style cheese
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup of red bell pepper
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Mix potaroes, butter, salt and black pepper
  3. Bake in oven 20 minutes
  4. Mix cheese, eggs and bell pepper
  5. Remove muffins from oven and top with bacon and egg mixture
